# VedaX-AI: Your Intelligent Healthcare Companion

## 🏥 Project Overview
**VedaX-AI** is a state-of-the-art, AI-enabled healthcare ecosystem designed to revolutionize how individuals interact with their health data and seek medical guidance. Built for the **Enigma'26 Hackathon**, it leverages advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) and intuitive design to provide accessible, efficient, and life-saving healthcare tools.

---

## 🛑 The Problem Statement
The current healthcare system is often fragmented and intimidating for the average patient, leading to several critical issues:

1.  **Triage Inefficiency**: Patients often cannot accurately judge the severity of their symptoms. This leads to **ER overcrowding** by low-risk patients and **delayed care** for those in high-risk categories.
2.  **Medical Information Gap**: Prescriptions and lab reports are filled with complex terminology. This **information asymmetry** causes patient anxiety and can lead to incorrect medication usage.
3.  **Fragmented Health Data**: Health data is typically scattered across various hospitals, apps, and physical papers, making it nearly impossible to maintain a **holistic view** of a patient's medical history.
4.  **Accessibility Barriers**: Traditional healthcare apps often lack **multilingual support** and hands-free capabilities, making them difficult to use for elderly patients or those in urgent distress.
5.  **Delayed Emergency Action**: In critical moments, people often don't know the **nearest appropriate clinic** or the immediate first-aid protocols to follow.

---

## ✅ Solutions Provided by VedaX-AI
VedaX-AI addresses these challenges through a suite of intelligent, user-centric features:

### 1. Smart AI Triage Engine
*   **Intelligent Assessment**: A conversational AI that asks clinical-grade follow-up questions to determine symptom severity.
*   **Risk Categorization**: Automatically tags cases as **Green** (Low), **Yellow** (Medium), or **Red** (High/Emergency) with clear, actionable advice.
*   **Location-Aware Care**: Uses real-time GPS data to recommend the **top 3 nearest specialized clinics** or hospitals instantly.

### 2. Prescription RAG (Intelligence-on-Demand)
*   **Smart Extraction**: Uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation to scan and interpret physical prescriptions and medical reports.
*   **Simplified Explanations**: Translates medical jargon into simple, everyday language and answers specific queries about dosage, side effects, and precautions.

### 3. Voice-Enabled Healthcare Assistant
*   **Hands-Free Interaction**: Full integration with **Voice-to-Text (STT)** and **Text-to-Speech (TTS)** allows users to interact with the app naturally.
*   **Multilingual Support**: Supports both **English and Hindi**, ensuring a wider reach and deeper impact in diverse communities.

### 4. Holistic Health Management
*   **EHR Management**: A centralized, secure hub for all medical records, ensuring that a patient's history is always at their fingertips.
*   **Medication Tracking**: Automated reminders and health-check prompts to maintain treatment consistency.

### 5. Emergency Protocol Integration
*   **Rapid Response**: For "Red" risk levels, the app provides immediate emergency protocols and can be configured to alert emergency services or emergency contacts.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
*   **Core**: React (Vite) + TypeScript
*   **AI Engine**: Google Gemini Pro (Text, Vision, and Multimodal)
*   **Styling**: Vanilla CSS + Tailwind CSS (Premium Glassmorphism & Dark Mode)
*   **Authentication**: Supabase Auth
*   **Backend**: Node.js / Express
*   **Deployment**: Render / Netlify
